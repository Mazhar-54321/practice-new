# Day 001 — Vocabulary

**Date:** 17-03-2026

---

| Word            | Meaning                                   | Example                                    |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Accomplish**  | to successfully finish something          | I want to accomplish my goals this year    |
| **Routine**     | a regular way of doing things daily       | My morning routine starts at 7am           |
| **Clarify**     | to make something easier to understand    | Can you clarify what you mean?             |
| **Efficient**   | doing something well without wasting time | She is an efficient worker                 |
| **Collaborate** | to work together with someone             | We collaborate with the backend team daily |

---

## My Sentences

| Word        | My Sentence                                                                         | Corrected                                                                                                  |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Accomplish  | I had accomplished my reading test before the power went off                        | I had accomplished my reading task before the power went off — _accomplish a task, not a test_             |
| Routine     | As a part of routine I walk 10,000 steps daily                                      | As a part of my routine I walk 10,000 steps daily — _add "my" before routine_                              |
| Clarify     | I urged interviewer to clarify the questions                                        | I requested the interviewer to clarify the questions — _"urged" is too strong, use "requested"_            |
| Efficient   | I am an efficient React developer                                                   | ✅ Perfect                                                                                                 |
| Collaborate | As a front-end Engineer I have to collaborate with backend team to complete my task | As a frontend engineer I have to collaborate with the backend team to complete my tasks — _tasks not task_ |
