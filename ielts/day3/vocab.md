# Day 003 — Vocabulary: Goals & Future Plans

**Date:** 22-03-2026

---

| Word           | Meaning                                    | Example 1                                             | Example 2                                           |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Achieve**    | to successfully reach a goal               | I want to achieve a band 6.5 in IELTS                 | She achieved her target within six months           |
| **Aspire**     | to have a strong desire to do something    | I aspire to become a senior engineer                  | He aspires to work at a top tech company            |
| **Commit**     | to dedicate yourself to something          | I am committed to learning every day                  | She committed to finishing the project on time      |
| **Progress**   | improvement over time                      | I can see my progress every week                      | Slow progress is still progress                     |
| **Milestone**  | an important achievement in a journey      | Completing Day 1 was a milestone for me               | We reached a major milestone this sprint            |
| **Overcome**   | to successfully deal with a challenge      | I want to overcome my fear of speaking English        | He overcame many obstacles to reach his goal        |
| **Consistent** | doing something regularly without stopping | Being consistent is more important than being perfect | She is consistent with her daily practice           |
| **Prioritise** | to decide what is most important           | I prioritise learning over entertainment              | We need to prioritise the critical bugs first       |
| **Dedicate**   | to give time and energy to something       | I dedicate three hours daily to studying              | He dedicated his weekends to building side projects |
| **Vision**     | a clear idea of what you want in future    | My vision is to be a well rounded engineer            | Having a vision keeps you focused                   |

## My Sentences

| Word       | My Sentence                                                                        | Corrected                                                                                              |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Achieve    | I will achieve my course target in 4 months                                        | ✅ Perfect                                                                                             |
| Aspire     | I aspired to become a skilled engineer                                             | I aspire to become a skilled engineer — _aspire is present tense, not past_                            |
| Commit     | Iam commited to learning frontend everyday                                         | I am committed to learning frontend every day — _committed spelling + every day is two words_          |
| Progress   | I started with a good progress in the course                                       | I have made good progress in the course — _progress is uncountable, no "a" before it_                  |
| Milestone  | Iam confident to complete first milestone this weekend                             | I am confident I will complete the first milestone this weekend — _missing "the" before milestone_     |
| Overcome   | I overcame the course-work balance by giving away watching youtube                 | I overcame the work-life balance challenge by giving up watching YouTube — _giving up not giving away_ |
| Consistent | To become a skilled developer I need to maintain consistency throughout the course | ✅ Perfect                                                                                             |
| Prioritise | I prioritized learning over other activities                                       | ✅ Perfect                                                                                             |
| Dedicate   | I have dedicated 3-4 hours daily for my course                                     | I have dedicated 3-4 hours daily to my course — _dedicated to, not for_                                |
| Vision     | My vision is to become a skilled engineer                                          | ✅ Perfect                                                                                             |
